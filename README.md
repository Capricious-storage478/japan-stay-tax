## ℹ️ About japan-stay-tax

japan-stay-tax helps you find the accommodation tax (宿泊税) for places across Japan. It covers many cities like Tokyo, Osaka, Kyoto, Niseko, and over 20 others. This app works with modern systems and uses TypeScript and JavaScript. It needs no extra software to run.

You can quickly learn how much tax to add to your stay for each city. This helps hosts, guests, and anyone involved in lodging in Japan.

## 🧰 Using japan-stay-tax

This app shows tax rules in a simple way. Here is how to use it:

1. Open the app after installation.
2. Select the city where you want to calculate the tax.
3. Enter the number of nights you will stay.
4. Enter the price per night or total lodging cost.
5. The app calculates the accommodation tax for your stay.
6. View the details about the tax rates used for the calculation.
7. You can reset or change inputs anytime.

The app works offline once installed, so you can use it without internet.
